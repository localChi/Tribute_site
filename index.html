Brief summary and timeline of Jesus's life

  • Jesus is born as the son of mary, conceived from the holy spirit
  • Jesus does his first miracle and turns water into wine
  • Jesus starts his ministry, and gets Andrew as his Apostle and his brother Simon Peter as his Disciple
  • Jesus goes around doing miracles and calling the rest of his 12 disciples for his ministry
  • Jesus is betrayed by his disciple Judas and is sentenced to death by the cross
  • Jesus dies on the cross and takes on the sins of the world:past,present,and future
  • Jesus ressurects and tells his disciples to spread the good news
  • Jesus is taken into heaven
